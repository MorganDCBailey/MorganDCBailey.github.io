About Me

A little bit about me:

Currently a level 4 student studying for a Computer Games Development Degree at Staffordshire University. An avid gamer that enjoys a large range of game types from first person shooters to racing.

My Projects

Marble Madness Prototype

A prototype game created for my Rapid Games Prototyping module, you play as a marble navigating levels and gaining points by moving as well as collecting coins.

Side-Scrolling Shooter Prototype

A prototype game created for my Rapid Games Prototyping module, you play as a viking with a turret mounted to his head. Your aim is to stay within view of the camera while fighting off waves of flying enemies.

Robo Puzzle

A game created for my Fundamentals of Game and Graphical System Development module, you play as a small rocket powered robot attempting to solve a maze while keeping your energy high.
