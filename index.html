About Me

A little bit about me:

Currently a level 4 student studying for a Computer Games Development Degree at Staffordshire University. An avid gamer that enjoys a large range of game types from first person shooters to racing.

My Projects

A look into the sorts of projects I have completed, or am currently creating.

A Video and further information on the below projects can be found on the "My Projects" Page.

Marble Madness Prototype

A prototype game created for my Rapid Games Prototyping module, you play as a marble navigating levels and gaining points by moving as well as collecting coins. Created in Unity.

Side-Scrolling Shooter Prototype

A prototype game created for my Rapid Games Prototyping module, you play as a viking with a turret mounted to his head. Your aim is to stay within view of the camera while fighting off waves of flying enemies. Created in Unity.

Robo Puzzle

A game created for my Fundamentals of Game and Graphical System Development module, you play as a small rocket powered robot attempting to solve a maze while keeping your energy high. Created from scratch with C++

Game Jams

A Video and further information on the below projects can be found on the "Games Jams" Page.

Spooky Shooter

A game created in the Freshers Week Game Jam and was created over 3 days, you play as a wizard shooting targets using a wand like the classic fair ground game it was inspired by.

The Plants Strike Back!

A game created during the 2023 Global Game Jam and was created through 5 days by 2 people, the theme was roots. You play as a person making their way through a forest when they are attacked by plants wanting vengance.
